About me

I grew up in Harlem, New York and I currently attend Cornell University. Almost all of my family immigrated from the Dominican Republic to the Bronx. I would spend every weekend as a kid with family in Castle Hill. Although I spent most of my summers in DR the first summer that I never went away to visit family is when I did the Girls Who Code Program at the New York City Microsoft Office. Girls Who Code made me feel like a career in tech was something that could be achieved for me and I will always be grateful for that experience!
